What areas do you cover?
GM Repointing is based in Bognor Regis and regularly works across West Sussex, including Chichester, Worthing, Brighton, Littlehampton, Arundel, Horsham, Crawley, and the surrounding area. If you are unsure whether we cover your location, give Garry a call and he will let you know.
How do I know if my pointing needs replacing?
Common signs include crumbling or cracked mortar joints, mortar that can be scratched out with a key, damp patches on interior walls, or visible gaps between bricks. If you are unsure, Garry can visit your property and give you an honest assessment at no charge.
Is the survey and quote truly free and no obligation?
Yes, completely. There is no charge for a site visit or written quote, and no obligation to proceed. Garry believes in being upfront with clients so you can make a confident decision without any pressure.
Are you fully insured?
Yes. GM Repointing is fully insured for all masonry restoration and brickwork services. You can book with complete confidence knowing your property is properly protected throughout every project.
Can you match the original mortar colour?
Mortar matching is an important part of the job, particularly on older and period properties. Garry takes care to assess the existing mix and colour before work begins, aiming for a result that blends naturally with the surrounding brickwork.
Do you work with both residential and commercial clients?
Yes. GM Repointing works with homeowners, landlords, property developers, and commercial clients. The same standard of workmanship and communication applies to every job, whether it is a single garden wall or a full commercial facade.
