What We Do

Our Specialist Masonry Services

Every service is carried out by Garry personally, ensuring consistent quality and precision from start to finish.

Brick Repointing

Specialist extraction and careful restoration of failing or deteriorated mortar joints, preventing water ingress and preserving the structural integrity of your property.

Perfect for

Homes and commercial buildings with crumbling, cracked, or receding mortar joints.

Brickwork Repairs

Expert masonry interventions including precise replacements and structural fixes for spalled, cracked, damaged, or loose bricks across all types of masonry.

Perfect for

Properties with spalled, broken, or structurally compromised brickwork that needs lasting repair.

Garden Walls

Dedicated structural repair and aesthetic restoration for exterior brick garden walls, keeping them safe, stable, and looking their best for years to come.

Perfect for

Homeowners with leaning, crumbling, or weathered garden and boundary walls needing attention.

Flintwork

Specialist traditional masonry skills for the precise installation, repair, and repointing of flintwork facades and structures, a craft requiring years of dedicated practice.

Perfect for

Period and rural properties with traditional flint facades, walls, or features in need of expert attention.

Ask Us Anything
What areas do you cover?
We are based in Bognor Regis and cover West Sussex and the surrounding area, including Chichester, Worthing, Littlehampton, Arundel, Selsey, Horsham, and many more towns across the county. If you are not sure whether we cover your postcode, give Garry a call and he will let you know.
How do I know if my pointing needs replacing?
Common signs include mortar joints that look recessed, crumbled, or cracked; damp patches appearing on interior walls; or loose bricks that move when pressed. If you are unsure, Garry can come and take a look free of charge and give you an honest assessment.
Do you offer free surveys and quotes?
Yes. Garry will visit your property to properly assess the brickwork and mortar condition, then provide a clear written quote. There is no charge for the survey and no obligation to proceed. You will know exactly what the work involves and what it will cost before anything starts.
Are you fully insured?
Yes. GM Repointing is fully insured for all masonry, repointing, and brickwork repair projects. You can book with confidence knowing your property is properly protected throughout.
Can you match the original mortar colour?
Yes. Garry takes great care to match the existing mortar colour and texture as closely as possible, particularly on period and older properties. He will assess the existing mix during the survey and discuss the options with you before work begins.
How long does repointing typically take?
It depends on the area and extent of the work. A single elevation on an average-sized house typically takes one to three days. Garry will give you an estimated timeframe as part of the quote so you can plan accordingly.
What causes mortar to fail?
The most common causes are weathering over time, frost damage, the use of cement-based mortar on older soft-brick buildings (which traps moisture and causes cracking), and general age-related deterioration. Addressing failing mortar early prevents more serious and costly structural problems later.
Do you work on both domestic and commercial properties?
Yes. Garry works with homeowners, landlords, and commercial property owners across West Sussex. Whether it is a single garden wall or a full commercial facade, each project receives the same careful approach and attention to detail.
